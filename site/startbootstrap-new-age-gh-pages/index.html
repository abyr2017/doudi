About Our Application

Alphabetic recognition refers to the ability to visually recognize letters of the alphabet. This includes the ability to name each letter and match that letter name with its written form, both upper and lower case, and in both manuscript and cursive. Alphabet recognition also includes the ability to distinguish between similar looking letters, like C/G, M/W, d/b and p/q. It is foundational to any alphabetic based language.

How to Find Us

Established in 1984, ENSI (the National School of Computer Science) is one of the most prestigious area of ​​engineering school specializing in computer Tunisia.

ENSI is a regional class engineering college where teaching and research - with relevance to the practical world as a guiding principle - continue to be its main goal. The mission of the ENSI is to advance knowledge and educate students in science, technology and other fields of study that will best serve Tunisia in the 21st century. The diplomas obtained in ENSI include Engineering degree in Computer Science , Degree of Master of Science and PhD degree in Computer Science .
